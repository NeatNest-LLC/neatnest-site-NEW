Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning FAQs

How much does carpet and upholstery cleaning cost?

Carpet and upholstery cleaning pricing depends on the number of rooms or furniture items, fabric type, level of soil, and overall condition. We provide free, no-obligation quotes for homeowners in Wallingford, North Haven, and Cheshire, CT so you know exactly what to expect before scheduling service.

How long do the carpets take to dry?

Most carpets dry within 4–8 hours after hot water extraction, depending on airflow, humidity, and carpet thickness. Upholstery typically dries within a few hours. We recommend increasing ventilation and limiting foot traffic until carpets are fully dry for best results.

Do you move furniture?

Yes. We move light furniture such as chairs, end tables, and ottomans as part of our carpet cleaning service. For heavier items like beds, dressers, or large entertainment centers, we ask that they be moved beforehand or we will clean around them. Protective pads are placed under furniture legs to help protect your carpet during the drying process.

Do you treat spots and high-traffic areas?

Yes. We pre-treat common spots and high-traffic areas before performing hot water extraction. This helps loosen soil and improve overall appearance. If you have specific stains or problem areas, let us know in advance so we can tailor the carpet cleaning process accordingly.

Is carpet and upholstery cleaning safe for pets and children?

Yes. Our carpet and upholstery cleaning process uses professional-grade solutions that are safe when applied and extracted properly. After cleaning and drying, your carpets and furniture are safe for children and pets to use as normal. We recommend allowing proper drying time and ventilation to ensure the best results.

How often should I have my carpets professionally cleaned?

Most carpet manufacturers recommend professional carpet cleaning every 12–18 months to help maintain warranty coverage and extend carpet life. Homes with pets, children, heavy foot traffic, or allergy concerns may benefit from cleaning every 6–12 months. Upholstery is typically refreshed every 1–2 years depending on use.
