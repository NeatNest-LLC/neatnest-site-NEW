Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning FAQs

How much does carpet and upholstery cleaning cost?

Pricing depends on the number of rooms or items, fabric type, and overall condition. We offer free, no-obligation quotes so you know exactly what to expect before scheduling.

How long do the carpets take to dry?

Most carpets dry within 4‚Äì8 hours depending on airflow, humidity, and carpet thickness. We recommend limiting foot traffic until fully dry. Upholstery typically dries in a few hours.

Do you move furniture?

Yes! We move light furniture (chairs, end tables, ottomans) as part of our service. For heavy furniture like beds, dressers, and entertainment centers, we ask that you move them beforehand or we can clean around them. We always place protective pads under furniture legs when moving items.

Do you treat spots and high-traffic areas?

Yes. We pre-treat and clean common spots and high-use areas as part of the service. If you have specific areas of concern, tell us in advance so we can plan accordingly.
