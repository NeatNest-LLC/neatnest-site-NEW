Top-to-Bottom Deep Cleaning

Our deep cleaning service is designed for homes that need more than a routine tidy-up. Whether you're preparing for a special event, recovering from a busy season, or simply want to refresh your space, we focus on the details that standard cleaning doesn't cover.

Unlike regular housekeeping, deep cleaning targets built-up grime, neglected surfaces, and hard-to-reach areas. We scrub baseboards, degrease kitchen surfaces, sanitize bathroom grout, dust ceiling fans and light fixtures, and restore your home to a like-new condition.

Deep Cleaning FAQs

How much does a deep cleaning cost in Wallingford CT?

Deep cleaning pricing varies based on home size, condition, and specific needs. Most homes in Wallingford, North Haven, and Cheshire range from $250–$600. We offer free, no-obligation quotes so you know exactly what to expect before we start.

How long does a deep clean take?

A typical deep clean takes 4–8 hours depending on the size of your home and the level of buildup. Larger homes or homes that haven't been professionally cleaned in a while may take longer. Our team works efficiently without cutting corners.

Do you bring supplies and equipment?

Yes! We bring all cleaning supplies, tools, and equipment needed for the job. We use professional-grade, eco-friendly products that are safe for your family and pets. If you have specific product preferences, just let us know.

What's the difference between deep cleaning and standard cleaning?

Standard cleaning focuses on maintenance—dusting, vacuuming, wiping surfaces, and keeping things tidy. Deep cleaning goes further: scrubbing grout, degreasing buildup, cleaning baseboards, dusting ceiling fans, and tackling neglected areas. It's a comprehensive reset for your home.

How often should I schedule deep cleaning?

Most homeowners schedule a deep clean 2–4 times per year, often with the change of seasons or before major holidays. If you also have regular maintenance cleaning, annual or semi-annual deep cleans are typically enough to keep your home in excellent condition.
