Our deep house cleaning service is designed for homes that need a full reset. We go beyond surface-level cleaning to address buildup in kitchens, bathrooms, baseboards, fixtures, and high-touch areas.

This service is ideal for spring cleaning, post-renovation cleanup, first-time clients, or homes that haven’t been professionally cleaned in a while. We focus on restoring freshness and bringing your home back to a truly clean baseline.

Deep Cleaning Focus Areas

KITCHEN

  • Cooking Area: Degrease stovetop and backsplash
  • Cabinets: Wipe down all fronts and handles
  • Countertops: Clean and sanitize all surfaces
  • Sink: Scrub basin and polish fixtures
  • Appliances: Clean exteriors (fridge, oven, dishwasher)
  • Detailing: Wipe baseboards, window sills, and trim
  • Floors: Sweep and mop thoroughly

BATHROOMS

  • Toilets: Scrub and disinfect (inside, base, and behind)
  • Sinks: Sanitize basins and countertops
  • Showers/Tubs: Full deep scrub, fixtures, and glass doors
  • Fixtures: Polish faucets and showerheads to remove scale
  • Cabinets: Wipe exterior fronts and hardware
  • Detailing: Wipe baseboards, door frames, and window sills
  • Floors: Sweep, mop, and sanitize floor surfaces

LIVING AREAS & BEDROOMS

  • Ceiling: Hand-wipe ceiling fans, glass, and fixtures
  • Woodwork: Wipe baseboards, door frames, and trim
  • Windows: Clean glass, sills, and bottom track
  • Surfaces: Dust all electronics and whip surfaces
  • Furniture: Vacuum upholstery and under cushions
  • Floors: Deep vacuum carpets and mop hard floors

Deep Cleaning FAQs

How much does a deep cleaning cost in Wallingford CT?

Deep cleaning in Wallingford, CT typically ranges from $250–$600 depending on your home’s size, condition, and level of buildup. Homes that haven’t been professionally cleaned in a while or require detailed scrubbing may be higher. We provide free, customized deep cleaning estimates for homeowners in Wallingford, North Haven, and Cheshire so you know exactly what to expect before we begin.

How long does a deep clean take?

Most deep cleaning services take 4–8 hours depending on square footage and the level of detail required. Larger homes or first-time deep cleans may take longer. Our team focuses on thorough, top-to-bottom cleaning without rushing or cutting corners.

What’s the difference between deep cleaning and recurring cleaning?

Recurring cleaning maintains cleanliness with regular dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and surface wipe-downs. Deep cleaning is more detailed and intensive, targeting buildup, grime, and neglected areas. Many homeowners schedule a deep clean before starting recurring cleaning service to create a clean baseline.

What's the difference between deep cleaning and standard cleaning?

Standard cleaning focuses on maintenance—dusting, vacuuming, wiping surfaces, and keeping things tidy. Deep cleaning goes further: scrubbing grout, degreasing buildup, cleaning baseboards, dusting ceiling fans, and tackling neglected areas. It's a comprehensive reset for your home.

How often should I schedule deep cleaning?

Most homeowners schedule a deep clean 2–4 times per year, often with the change of seasons or before major holidays. If you also have regular maintenance cleaning, annual or semi-annual deep cleans are typically enough to keep your home in excellent condition.

Is deep cleaning recommended before starting recurring service?

Yes. Many homeowners in Wallingford choose a deep clean before beginning weekly or bi-weekly cleaning. A deep cleaning establishes a fresh starting point so ongoing maintenance cleaning is more effective and consistent.
