Routine Housekeeping

Our regular housekeeping service includes weekly or bi-weekly cleaning of all main living areas. We'll dust surfaces, vacuum carpets and rugs, mop floors, clean bathrooms thoroughly, sanitize kitchen areas, and maintain your home's overall cleanliness with consistent, reliable service.

×

Top-to-Bottom Deep Clean

Our comprehensive deep cleaning service goes beyond regular housekeeping. We clean baseboards, window sills, light fixtures, ceiling fans, inside appliances (oven, refrigerator, microwave), scrub grout and tile, wipe down walls, and tackle those hard-to-reach areas that accumulate dirt over time.

×

Move-In / Move-Out Clean

Perfect for moving transitions, this service ensures your new home is spotless before you move in, or your old home is pristine for the next occupants. Includes deep cleaning of all rooms, cabinets, appliances, bathrooms, and removing any residue left behind.

×

Office & Commercial Cleaning

Professional cleaning services for offices, retail spaces, and commercial buildings. We offer flexible scheduling including after-hours cleaning, desk and workspace sanitization, restroom maintenance, floor care, and common area cleaning to maintain a professional environment.

Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning

Professional steam cleaning for carpets, rugs, and upholstered furniture. Our service includes stain removal, odor treatment, fabric protection application, and deep extraction cleaning that removes embedded dirt, allergens, and bacteria for a healthier living environment.
