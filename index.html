Routine Housekeeping

Our regular housekeeping service includes weekly or bi-weekly cleaning of all main living areas. We'll dust surfaces, vacuum carpets and rugs, mop floors, clean bathrooms thoroughly, sanitize kitchen surfaces, and take out trash. Perfect for maintaining a clean and healthy home environment.

×

Top-to-Bottom Deep Clean

Our comprehensive deep cleaning service goes beyond regular housekeeping. We clean baseboards, window sills, light fixtures, ceiling fans, inside appliances (oven, refrigerator, microwave), scrub tile grout, wash walls, and clean inside cabinets and drawers. Ideal for seasonal cleaning or preparing for special events.

Move-In / Move-Out Clean

Perfect for moving transitions, this service ensures your new home is spotless before you move in, or your old home is pristine for the next occupants. Includes deep cleaning of all rooms, inside and outside of all appliances, cabinets, closets, and thorough sanitization.

Office & Commercial Cleaning
