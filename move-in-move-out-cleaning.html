Professional Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

Whether you’re moving into a new home or preparing one for sale, our move-in/move-out cleaning ensures the property is spotless and ready for its next chapter.

Unlike standard cleaning, move-in/move-out service covers every surface, appliance, and corner of an empty home. We clean inside cabinets and closets, scrub appliances inside and out, sanitize bathrooms from top to bottom, and leave the property move-in ready or inspection-ready.

We serve homeowners, renters, landlords, and real estate agents throughout Wallingford, North Haven, and Cheshire.

What's Included in Our Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning

KITCHEN: TOTAL SANITIZATION

  • Appliances: Deep clean inside and out (fridge, oven, microwave, dishwasher)
  • Cooking Area: Degrease stovetop, backsplash, and range hood filter
  • Cabinetry: Detailed wipe of all fronts, interior shelves, and drawers
  • Surfaces: Clean and sanitize all countertops
  • Sink: Scrub basin and polish fixtures to a high shine
  • Detailing: Clean baseboards, window sills, and light fixtures
  • Floors: Intensive sweep and mop; remove all debris/trash

BATHROOMS: DEEP RESTORATION

  • Commode: Scrub and disinfect toilets (tank, base, and behind)
  • Lavatory: Sanitize sinks, countertops, and faucets
  • Showers/Tubs: Intensive scrub of tiles, grout, and basins
  • Mirrors: Polish mirrors and all chrome fittings
  • Cabinetry: Wipe inside and outside of vanities and drawers
  • Detailing: Clean baseboards, window sills, and light fixtures
  • Floors: Sweep and mop thoroughly; remove all trash

BEDROOMS & LIVING AREAS: THE FRESH START

  • High-Dusting: Hand-wipe ceiling fans, light fixtures, and HVAC vents
  • Woodwork: Detail all baseboards and door frames
  • Windows: Clean sills, tracks, and blinds/shutters
  • Closets: Wipe down all shelves, rods, and floor tracks
  • Floors: Power-vacuum carpets and rugs; sweep and mop hard floors
  • Final Touch: Remove any remaining debris or trash

ADDITIONAL SERVICES & ADD-ONS

  • Laundry Room: Clean inside washer/dryer, cabinets, and floors
  • Utility Areas: Sweep and spot-clean basement or garage
  • Traffic Areas: Detailed cleaning of entryways, hallways, and stair railings
  • Walls: Touch-up cleaning for scuffs and marks
  • Interior Windows: Professional glass and track cleaning (Add-on)

Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning FAQs

How much does move-out cleaning cost in Wallingford CT?

Move-in and move-out cleaning in Wallingford, CT typically ranges from $300–$600 depending on the size of the home, overall condition, and level of detail required. Larger properties or homes with heavy buildup may fall on the higher end. We provide free, customized move-out cleaning quotes for homeowners, renters, landlords, and real estate agents in Wallingford, North Haven, and Cheshire so you know exactly what to expect.

How long does move-in/move-out cleaning take?

Most move-in or move-out cleaning services take 3–5 hours depending on the property size and condition. Empty homes allow for more detailed cleaning inside cabinets, closets, appliances, and along baseboards. Our team focuses on thorough, top-to-bottom cleaning to prepare the home for its next occupant.

Is this for empty homes only?

Move-in and move-out cleaning is most common for empty properties, but we can also provide detailed cleaning for occupied homes during a transition. Whether you're preparing a home for new buyers, tenants, or moving into a property yourself, we tailor the service to the situation.

What's the difference between move-out cleaning and deep cleaning?

Move-out cleaning is designed specifically for vacant properties and includes detailed cleaning inside cabinets, drawers, closets, appliances, and other fully accessible areas. Deep cleaning focuses on occupied homes and addresses buildup, baseboards, fixtures, and high-touch surfaces. Move-out cleaning is more transition-focused, while deep cleaning is maintenance-focused.

Can you work with my moving schedule?

Yes. We understand that moving timelines in Wallingford and surrounding towns can change quickly. We offer flexible scheduling for move-in and move-out cleaning and do our best to accommodate tight deadlines so your property is ready for closing, inspection, or handoff.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking move-in or move-out cleaning 1–2 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date, especially during busy moving seasons. If you need last-minute service, contact us and we’ll do our best to fit you into our schedule.
