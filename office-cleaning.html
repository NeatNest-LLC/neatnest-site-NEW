Professional Office & Commercial Cleaning

A clean workplace creates a professional impression, boosts employee morale, and promotes a healthier environment. Our commercial cleaning service is designed for offices, retail spaces, medical facilities, and other businesses throughout Wallingford, North Haven, and Cheshire CT. We provide thorough, reliable cleaning on a schedule that works for you—daily, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.

We provide professional office and commercial cleaning services throughout Wallingford, North Haven, and Cheshire CT. As a locally owned company based in Wallingford, we offer reliable scheduling and consistent service across all three towns.

What's Included in Our Office & Commercial Cleaning

OFFICES & WORKSPACES

  • Workstations: Dust and sanitize desks, monitors, and surfaces
  • Electronics: Sanitize high-touch phones, keyboards, and mice
  • Storage: Dust filing cabinets, bookshelves, and credenzas
  • Touchpoints: Disinfect light switches and door handles
  • Floors: Vacuum carpets and rugs; sweep and mop hard floors
  • Waste: Empty bins and replace liners

RESTROOMS

  • Sanitization: Scrub and disinfect toilets and urinals
  • Lavatory: Sanitize sinks, countertops, and partitions
  • Fixtures: Polish mirrors and faucets
  • Replenishing: Refill soap dispensers, paper towels, and tissue
  • Disinfection: Deep clean all high-touch surfaces and walls
  • Floors: Sweep and mop with hospital-grade disinfectant
  • Trash: Empty waste receptacles and replace liners

BREAK ROOMS & KITCHENS

  • Surfaces: Clean and sanitize all tables and countertops
  • Appliances: Wipe down exterior of fridge, microwave, and coffee stations
  • Sink: Scrub basin and polish fixtures
  • Cabinetry: Wipe exterior fronts and handles
  • Common Areas: Straighten chairs and organize shared spaces
  • Floors: Sweep and mop to remove spills and debris
  • Waste: Empty trash and recycling bins

LOBBIES & COMMON AREAS

  • First Impression: Dust reception desks and waiting area furniture
  • Glass: Clean entrance doors and interior partition windows
  • Safety: Wipe down handrails, elevator buttons, and light switches
  • Detailing: Spot-clean walls, baseboards, and door frames
  • Floors: Vacuum entry mats and hallways; sweep and mop hard floors
  • Maintenance: Ensure a spotless, professional appearance throughout

Office & Commercial Cleaning FAQs

How much does office cleaning cost in Wallingford, CT?

Office cleaning costs in Wallingford, CT typically range from $150–$500 per visit for small offices (1,000–3,000 sq ft), depending on square footage, cleaning frequency, and services needed. We provide customized commercial cleaning quotes for businesses in Wallingford, North Haven, and Cheshire. Contact us for a free estimate tailored to your facility.

What hours do you provide commercial cleaning?

We offer flexible office cleaning schedules in Wallingford and surrounding towns. Most businesses choose after-hours cleaning (evenings or weekends), but we also provide early morning or off-peak commercial cleaning to avoid disrupting daily operations.

Do you bring your own commercial cleaning supplies?

Yes. Our office cleaning team brings all professional-grade cleaning supplies, equipment, and tools needed for your facility. We use safe, effective commercial products and can accommodate eco-friendly or fragrance-free requests.

Are your office cleaners insured and background-checked?

Absolutely. All our team members are background-checked, trained, and insured. NeatNest LLC is fully bonded and insured for commercial work, giving you complete peace of mind when we're in your facility.

How often should my office be professionally cleaned?

Cleaning frequency depends on foot traffic, number of employees, and the type of business. Most small offices in Wallingford choose weekly or bi-weekly office cleaning, while higher-traffic workplaces may benefit from multiple visits per week. We help you determine the ideal commercial cleaning schedule to maintain a healthy, presentable environment year-round.

How do you ensure quality and consistency with recurring office cleaning?

We follow a detailed commercial cleaning checklist tailored to your office layout and service plan. Each visit includes consistent task tracking, high-touch surface disinfection, restroom sanitation, trash removal, and floor care. As a locally owned office cleaning company serving Wallingford, North Haven, and Cheshire, CT, we prioritize reliability, clear communication, and consistent results so your workplace always looks professional and well-maintained.
