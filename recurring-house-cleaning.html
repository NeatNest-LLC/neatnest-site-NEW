  • Clean and shine sink and fixtures
  • Clean stovetop and range hood

BATHROOMS

Recurring House Cleaning FAQs

How much does recurring cleaning cost in Wallingford CT?

Recurring cleaning pricing varies based on home size, frequency, and condition. Most homes range from $120‚Äì$350 per visit. Weekly service offers the best per-visit rate, while monthly service costs slightly more per visit. We offer free, no-obligation quotes so you know exactly what to expect.

Do I need to sign a contract?

No long-term contracts required! We work on a recurring schedule with flexible cancellation. If you need to skip a cleaning, reschedule, or pause service, just give us reasonable notice. We want you to stay because you love the service, not because you're locked in.

Do prices change each visit?

No, pricing remains consistent unless scope changes.

What if I need to reschedule or skip a cleaning?

Life happens! Just give us at least 24-48 hours notice and we'll reschedule your cleaning to a time that works better. We understand vacations, sick days, and last-minute changes‚Äîwe're flexible and work around your life.

No! Most recurring clients give us a key or door code so we can clean while they're at work. We're fully insured, bonded, and background-checked. Many clients prefer coming home to a freshly cleaned house rather than being there during service.

How often should I schedule service?

Bi-weekly is most common; weekly for busy households or monthly for less busy homes.
